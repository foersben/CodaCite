# Reflect and learn

## Step 1
Analyze the most recent interaction where the user corrected your behavior or where a script/test failed due to your code. 

## Step 2
Extract the core misunderstanding. Formulate a single, generalized, preventative rule.

## Step 3
Use your filesystem tools to append this new rule to `.agents/rules/04-lessons-learned.md` under the "Accumulated Lessons" section. Ensure the formatting matches the existing list. 

## Step 4
Output a brief confirmation message to the user stating what new rule has been permanently learned.
